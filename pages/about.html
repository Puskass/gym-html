About Us

GymQR is a web application that revolutionizes the way people approach and engage in gym workouts. By scanning QR codes for quick access, GymQR eliminates the need for perfectly executed exercises. Simply scan the QR code near the gym equipment, and you'll instantly receive visual instructions on how to perform the exercise correctly.

Clear and visual instructions are presented through short YouTube videos to ensure clarity and precision.

Our partnership with gyms ensures an integrated experience with fitness centers.

No need to install an app; GymQR is accessible via a web browser, making access and workouts hassle-free.
